# Acme Corp Marketing Strategy & Channels

## 1. Overview
Our GTM strategy is currently based on high-intent developer acquisition via search and developer advocacy.
* **Target Audience:** Tech leads, Software Engineers, DevOps teams, and Solo Founders.
* **Primary Acquisition Channels:** Google Ads, Developer newsletters, GitHub sponsor sponsorships, and SEO.

## 2. Channel Performance Metrics (Last Quarter)
* **Search Engine Ads (Google/Bing):** 
  - Cost Per Click (CPC): $2.40
  - Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): $95.00
  - Trial-to-Paid Conversion: 3.2%
* **Sponsorships & Newsletters:**
  - Flat rate: $5,000 / run
  - Trial-to-Paid Conversion: 2.1%
  - Average Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $110.00
* **Organic (Developer Advocacy & SEO):**
  - Average CAC: $15.00 (content creation costs)
  - Trial-to-Paid Conversion: 4.5%

## 3. Freemium Plan Considerations
We are considering introducing a freemium tier (currently 14-day free trial only).
* **Opportunity:** High top-of-funnel (ToF) growth. Developers prefer immediate product usage without entering credit card info. We expect a 4x increase in signups.
* **Risk (Brand Value & Gating):** If the free tier is too generous, it will cannibalize our existing $29/mo starter plan. We must gate features like advanced analytics, multi-workspace collaboration, and high-frequency webhook pipelines to avoid self-cannibalization.
* **Competitor Benchmarks:** Most competitors (e.g. Virtual C-Suite, Cofounder.co) offer limited free tiers with visual reminders to upgrade. Self-cannibalization averages 15-22% of baseline plans if gating is too loose.
