# Acme Corp Team Operations & Support Capacity

## 1. Team Structure & Headcount
* **Engineering:** 6 developers (4 full-stack, 1 DevOps, 1 Lead).
* **Customer Support:** 2 full-time agents (handling tier-1 and tier-2 tickets).
* **Product & Design:** 1 PM, 1 Designer.
* **Operations:** 1 Chief Operations Officer (managing vendors, runway, and compliance).

## 2. Current Project Load & Bandwidth
* **Engineering Bandwidth:** 100% committed to security compliance (SOC 2) and API gateway refactoring for the next 2 months. 
* **Support Load:** Currently averages 45 tickets/day. Support response time is under 1.5 hours.
* **Support Bottleneck:** Support agents spend 35% of their time manually resetting passwords and configuring custom integrations for customers due to lack of self-service tooling.

## 3. Operational Impact of Freemium
* **Ticket Volumetrics:** Based on similar SaaS rollouts, launching a freemium plan increases user signups by 300-400%, which translates to a projected increase of 120-150 support tickets/day from non-paying users.
* **Capacity Shortfall:** Our current support team of 2 cannot handle >70 tickets/day without ticket response times slipping past 24 hours.
* **GTM Requirement:** Before rolling out freemium, we must:
  - Build automated self-service password recovery.
  - Implement a Discord community channel for free users to crowd-source tier-1 troubleshooting, keeping our ticketing system reserved strictly for paying Pro customers.
  - Allocate budget to hire 1 additional support contractor ($45,000 / year).
